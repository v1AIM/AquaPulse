# AquaPulse 🌊

**Stop Guessing. Start Knowing. Own Your Health.**

AquaPulse is the world's first fully integrated health ecosystem combining wearable biometrics, smart hydration tracking, and AI-powered insights to give you total control over your body.

## ✨ Features

### 🎯 Core Components

- **AP-1 Wearable Band** - 24/7 biometric monitoring with 47 health metrics
  - Medical-grade sensors in a 28g ultra-slim form
  - 7-day battery life
  - 96% clinical accuracy
  - Real-time HRV, sleep architecture, and recovery tracking

- **AP Hydro Smart Bottle** - Intelligent hydration tracking
  - ±2ml precision ultrasonic sensors
  - Smart reminders adapt to strain and temperature
  - 600ml capacity with 30-day battery
  - LED ring status indicator

- **AquaPulse OS** - AI-powered health intelligence platform
  - Daily Recovery Score (0-100)
  - Predictive performance modeling
  - Cross-device AI correlation
  - Personalized health coaching
  - Available on iOS, Android & Web

### 📊 Health Metrics Tracked

- Heart Rate & HRV (Heart Rate Variability)
- Sleep Quality & Architecture (REM, Deep, Light cycles)
- Recovery & Strain Scores
- Blood Oxygen (SpO₂)
- Stress Levels
- Hydration Status
- And 40+ more biometric measurements
